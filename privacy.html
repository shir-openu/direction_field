Privacy Policy

ODE Direction Field Visualizer

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

ODE Direction Field Visualizer is a mathematical visualization tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All computations are performed locally on your device.

No Account Required

The app does not require registration, login, or any personal information to use.

No Analytics

The app does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

No Network Access Required

The app functions fully offline. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

Sharing Feature

The app includes an optional share feature that uses your device's native share dialog. Any data shared (equations, images) is handled by the apps you choose to share with, not by us.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is an educational mathematics tool suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
